About Me

A NYC-based software engineer, My passion came out through curiosity when I spoke to a friend of mine who builds websites for a living. Ever since that day I wanted to know how did that work. I decided to teach myself the basics about data structure and decided to further my education by joining App Academy Bootcamp Prep course where I learn about the fundamentals of Javascript. After completing the curriculum I decided this will be my career. So I left my full-time job at Salvatore Ferragamo and joined a full-time 16-week immersive Bootcamp program at App Academy. This was a big decision for me but I'm glad to say that I graduated from an intensive Bootcamp with a < 3% acceptance rate. I learn a lot from working with a small team to creating a full website.
